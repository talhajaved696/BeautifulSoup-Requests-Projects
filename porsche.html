About Porsche Centre Vancouver

Porsche Centre Vancouver's 40,000 square foot dealership is located at 688 Terminal Avenue. The new facility features a large, modern showroom, a private fitting lounge where customers can experience Porsche product customization, and a customer cafe. The service centre offers increased capacity with 20 technologically advanced service bays, specialized vehicle lifts, and a drive through service and valet service and area for the optimal service experience.Our flagship Porsche Centre stocks Vancouver's only certifiednew Porscheandpre-owned Porschecollection. Choose fromPorsche 911,Porsche Cayman,Porsche Boxster,Porsche Panamera,Porsche Cayenne, and the all-newPorsche Macanmodels.
